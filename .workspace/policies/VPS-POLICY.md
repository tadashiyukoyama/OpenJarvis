A VPS is not contracted, provisioned or accessed until a separate explicit
authorization names the host and scope. Any future release must use
SHA-addressed artifacts, a current symlink, shared data and an explicit
deployment-state manifest. This policy does not authorize a host, account,
secret, connection or deployment.
